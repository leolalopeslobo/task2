A GLIMPSE OF US

LA Mercator is a fast growing firm located in Goa, India providing a comprehensive platform for foreign trade. We are committed to tapping on business opportunities by connecting Indian companies with the rest of the world. With an objective to shorten geographical distances and develop commercial ties, LA Mercator contributes to the competitiveness of your company by being the most reliable partner in expanding your business beyond borders.

OUR SERVICES

IMPORT

Businesses should conduct preliminary research on the goods they want to import from other countries before embarking on an import journey. We provide customized solutions to match your business demands by

  • conducting market research
  • finding global suppliers
  • providing guidance on government regulations & trade laws
  • tax classification of goods
  • monitoring documentation for custom clearance
  • contracting insurance
  • contracting national & international freight
  • assisting in exchange contracts

EXPORT

Do you want to accelerate the growth of your business? Have you thought about the advantages that exporting can add to your business?

A quality product should not be restricted only to the borders of a country. Our job is to determine the value for your business and to find the perfect buyer by

  • providing up-to-date market knowledge
  • identifying business opportunities overseas
  • evaluating international market sales prospects
  • developing distribution strategies
  • providing solutions for restrictions imposed by targetted country’s government & tariffs
  • coordinating of transport and release of goods
